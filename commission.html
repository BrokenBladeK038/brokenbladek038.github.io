How to Order a piece, and how the process works:

There are multiple ways to contact me to order a commission. None of them are quicker or more likely to get accepted than the others.

  1. Use the "Contact" tab here on my website, and select the "commission" option from the dropdown list. I will reply within 1-2 days with a form to fill out if I am open and accepting commissions at that time.
  2. Use the link at the top of this page and fill out the form. If the form is closed, wait until I'm open and accepting again.
  3. Note or PM me on the various websites I'm hosting my work on. DeviantArt, FurAffinity, Weasyl, Tumblr, twitter ETC and a form will be given to you if I'm open.

  1. Once the form has been successfully filled out, I will email you to confirm your order and ask any questions I may need in order to properly complete it. 
  2. I will send you a PayPal invoice, and you will get put down on my To-Do list.
  3. I do not start your commission until it has been paid in full.
  4. If you request Work in Progress shots (WIPS) I will email you with Sketch, Lines, + Flats only.
  5. I will send you preview sized images until you have 100% confirmed you like it. A web sized version, and a high resolution will follow only after I get your confirmation.
